# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a fully responsive single-page landing page for the "Smart Placement Ecosystem" AI-powered placement platform with a modern SaaS dark gradient theme.

**Planned changes:**
- Sticky Navbar with logo image, navigation links (Home, About, How It Works, Contact), Login/Register buttons, hamburger menu on mobile, and background change on scroll
- Hero Section with headline "AI-Powered Smart Placement Platform", subtitle, "Get Started" and "Login" CTA buttons, and a generated hero background image
- About Section describing the problem with traditional placement, AI-based improvements, and benefits for students and recruiters
- "How It Works" Section with 4 sequential step cards (upload resume, AI analyzes skills, match jobs, recruiter shortlists), each with step number, icon, title, and description
- Statistics Section with 4 static stat cards: number of students, partner companies, total applications, and placement success rate
- Contact Section with heading, invite message, and contact details or a placeholder form
- Footer with quick links, contact information, and brand name
- Cohesive dark navy-to-teal gradient theme with card-based layout, consistent typography, and gradient-styled buttons throughout
- Full responsiveness across desktop, tablet, and mobile using Tailwind CSS breakpoints
- Serve generated hero background and logo as static assets used in Hero Section and Navbar
